What is this?

This is Velocette. It's a personal task and velocity tracker. You can quickly and easily add tasks to a list, and mark them as complete. Velocette tracks the time that you complete tasks, and provides a list of tasks that were completed in a given time period?

Why is this?

I work on a team that practices Scrum. Part of the scrum process is a daily morning meeting in which team members share what they completed the previous day, and what they plan on working on today. I have plenty of tools for tracking tasks, but I wanted to see a list of just what was completed the previous day so that I knew exactly what to report at the scrum.
